HYPOT

Return the hypotenuse of the right triangle with other two sides of the given lengths.

Synopsis

hypot <x> <y>

Arguments

<x> - length of one perpendicular side

<y> - length of the other perpendicular side

    Returns

    Return the hypotenuse of the right triangle with other two sides of lengths(<x> and <y>).

    Example

              make “foo 3
          make “bar hypot :foo 3    ; bar == 5

    See Also

    acos asin atan cos cosh sin sinh tan tanh
